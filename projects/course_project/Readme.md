

# **CSwithShahil: Free Online Courses Platform**

Welcome to **CSwithShahil**, an online platform where we host all our free **courses** and **recordings**. Our goal is to provide **quality education** in fields like **Programming**, **AI**, **Machine Learning**, and **Data Science**. All content is **completely free** for learners around the world.

![CSwithShahil Screenshot](screenshot-placeholder.jpg)
*Above: Sample of our online platform with courses and playlists*

## **Features**

### 1. **Free Courses & Recordings**

At **CSwithShahil**, we offer free access to **course videos** hosted on YouTube. Browse through different categories and watch complete course recordings at no cost.

### 2. **Course Categories**

We organize courses into distinct categories for better navigation:

* **Forms**: Access important forms related to courses, such as registration, feedback, and enrollment.
* **Course Materials**: Download course PDFs, presentations, and other educational resources.
* **Feedback**: Submit feedback on courses or rate the instructor's performance.
* **Review**: Read and write course reviews based on your experience.

### 3. **YouTube Playlist Integration**

Our courses are curated and presented through YouTube **playlists**, which we directly integrate with the platform. Each playlist contains organized lessons with descriptions and videos that you can access anytime.

### 4. **Admin Panel for Course Management**

An **admin panel** is available for managing course data, feedback, and reviews. This system enables efficient handling of course material and ensures the platform remains up-to-date with the latest content.

### 5. **Sticky Navigation Bar**

The website features a **sticky navigation bar**, allowing easy access to different sections, such as:

* Home
* About Us
* Contact
* Support

### 6. **Interactive Sidebar**

The sidebar allows you to filter courses by **category** (such as **Personality Development**, **Data Science**, etc.) and navigate through relevant course materials, forms, feedback, and reviews.

### 7. **Smooth Scrolling Navigation**

Navigate through the platform with smooth scrolling, making it easy to switch between sections without any abrupt jumps. The navigation bar stays at the top for quick access to important sections.

## **How to Use**

### Step 1: **Browse Available Categories**

* Select a **category** from the sidebar (e.g., **Course Materials**, **Forms**, etc.).
* Explore related links for downloading course materials, accessing forms, or reading reviews.

### Step 2: **Watch Videos**

* Go to **Home**, where you can select a **YouTube playlist** from the list.
* Click on any playlist title, and it will open the list of **course videos**.
* Select a video to start watching. Each video will be embedded within the page.

### Step 3: **Provide Feedback or Reviews**

* After completing the course, you can submit feedback or rate the course and instructor directly from the **Feedback** section in the sidebar.
